How to Create a Favicon from a Logo Online

📅 2026-06-16⏱ 5 min read✍ ToolsNowPro Team

A favicon is the tiny icon that appears in browser tabs, bookmarks, and shortcuts. It is small, but it gives a site a finished look and makes the brand easier to recognize at a glance.

The fastest workflow is to take a simple logo or mark and turn it into a favicon that still looks clear at tiny sizes. That means stripping away extra detail and keeping the design bold.

Why a simple favicon works best

Detailed artwork often collapses into noise when it is reduced to tab size. A clean icon, letter mark, or symbol is easier to identify and looks sharper in the browser.

Good favicons also help with bookmarks and mobile home screen shortcuts. Even though the image is small, it still supports brand recognition every time the user sees your tab.

How to make one from a logo

  • Start with a simple logo, symbol, or initial.
  • Remove small details that disappear at 16x16 size.
  • Generate or export a favicon-friendly file and test it in a tab.

If the favicon looks crowded, simplify the source image before you generate the final version. The result should be readable instantly, not impressive in high resolution only.

What to check before publishing

Make sure the icon works on light and dark browser tabs, still looks clean in bookmarks, and matches your brand colors. A favicon generator makes that workflow much faster than manual export settings.
