Lorem Ipsum is still the fastest way to fill a layout when you are designing a landing page, a dashboard, or a client mockup. Instead of writing real copy too early, you can generate clean placeholder text and keep the design process moving.

A good generator saves time because it produces realistic paragraph lengths without distracting from the structure of the page. That makes it easier to test typography, spacing, and section flow before the final content is ready.

Why placeholder text still matters

Real copy can slow down the design process when the message is not finalized. Placeholder text solves that problem by giving you something neutral to work with while you review layout, hierarchy, and visual balance.

It is especially useful when you need to show a draft to a client or teammate. The page feels complete enough to review, but nobody confuses the sample text for final content.

How to use the Lorem Ipsum generator

  • Choose the amount of text you need for the layout.
  • Generate paragraphs, sentences, or short blocks for the mockup.
  • Copy the result into your design file, CMS, or prototype.

If you need a quick draft for testing spacing, the free generator is faster than pasting the same text over and over by hand. It keeps your workflow simple and repeatable.

Common use cases

Designers use placeholder text for wireframes, blog layouts, app previews, and marketing pages. Developers also use it when they want to build a section before the final copy has been approved.
