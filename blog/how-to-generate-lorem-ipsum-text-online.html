How to Generate Lorem Ipsum Text Online – Step by Step

📅 2026-06-15⏱ 5 min read✍ ToolsNowPro Team

If you need placeholder text quickly, the easiest method is to use a Lorem Ipsum generator instead of copying the same sentence repeatedly. That gives you a cleaner mockup and saves time when you are building the layout.

The process is simple: decide how much text you need, generate it, and paste it into your design or content area. The generator handles the filler copy so you can focus on spacing, typography, and structure.

Step 1: Decide the text length

Start with the size of the section you are filling. A hero block might only need a few lines, while a blog layout may need multiple paragraphs. Matching the length to the layout makes the draft feel more realistic.

Step 2: Generate the placeholder copy

Use the free online generator to create text in seconds. You can make short snippets, longer paragraphs, or enough content to test a full page layout without writing anything yourself.

Step 3: Paste it into your mockup

Copy the output into your prototype, CMS draft, or design file. Then preview the page and see how the typography, spacing, and line breaks look in a more realistic setting.
