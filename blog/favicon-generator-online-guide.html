Favicon Generator Online: Create a Website Icon in Minutes

📅 2026-06-16⏱ 5 min read✍ ToolsNowPro Team

A favicon is the tiny icon that appears in browser tabs, bookmarks, and search results. It is small, but it makes a site feel finished and easier to recognize at a glance.

A favicon generator helps you turn a logo or simple mark into the right size and format without fiddling with export settings. That is useful when you want something clean quickly, especially for a new site or side project.

Why favicons matter

Favicons improve recognition. When someone has multiple tabs open, the icon helps them find your site faster. It also makes the bookmark bar look more polished and can help a brand feel more established.

Even though the icon is small, it should still be readable. Simple shapes and bold contrast usually work better than tiny details that disappear at tab size.

How to make a favicon

  • Start with a simple logo, letter mark, or symbol.
  • Export or generate it at the right size for browser use.
  • Upload the output to your site and test it in a tab.

If the design feels busy, reduce detail before generating the final file. The best favicons are often the simplest ones.

Good favicon habits

Use high contrast, check it on a dark and light tab, and make sure it still looks clear at 16x16 size. A favicon generator is a quick way to test all of that without guessing.
