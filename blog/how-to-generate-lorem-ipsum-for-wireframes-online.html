How to Generate Lorem Ipsum for Wireframes Online

📅 2026-06-16⏱ 5 min read✍ ToolsNowPro Team

Wireframes are easier to review when they look like a real page, even before the final copy exists. Lorem Ipsum gives you that structure without forcing you to write placeholder content by hand.

For wireframes, the goal is not to create polished copy. The goal is to test spacing, hierarchy, and layout speed. A generator helps you do that in a few seconds.

Why Lorem Ipsum still works for wireframes

Design drafts often need text blocks that feel realistic enough to show how the layout behaves. Lorem Ipsum is neutral, so it keeps attention on spacing, line length, and content flow instead of the actual words.

That is especially useful in Figma, web mockups, and landing page previews where you want the section to feel complete without writing final copy too early.

How to generate it the right way

  • Pick the amount of text that matches the wireframe section.
  • Generate enough copy to simulate the final layout.
  • Paste it into your draft and preview the page.

If the section is a hero, keep the text short. If it is a content block or blog preview, use more paragraphs so the layout behaves more like the finished page.

When to use placeholder text

Use it during early design, client review, or development when the real content is not ready yet. It keeps the project moving while the final wording is still being worked out.
