What Is Lorem Ipsum Placeholder Text? Simple Guide

📅 2026-06-15⏱ 4 min read✍ ToolsNowPro Team

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used to fill a design when the final copy is not ready yet. It gives a realistic shape to paragraphs without drawing attention to the actual words.

The text comes from a scrambled Latin passage that has been used in printing and design for decades. Today it is most common in website mockups, app screens, and layout drafts.

Why designers use it

Placeholder text helps you judge how a page will look once real content is added. It is useful for testing line length, spacing, alignment, and overall rhythm on the page.

That makes it a simple but important tool for anyone creating mockups quickly. You can focus on the structure first and polish the wording later.

When to replace it

Replace Lorem Ipsum as soon as real copy is ready. The goal is to use it only as a temporary stand-in so the final design does not ship with meaningless filler text.
