Bio Writing Tips

  • Lead with value — tell visitors what they get from following you within the first 5 words
  • Use keywords — Instagram and TikTok index bio text, so include your niche words
  • Add a CTA — "New video every week", "DMs open", or a link invite
  • Match your vibe — an aesthetic account should have an aesthetic bio; a comedy account can be funny
  • Keep it scannable — line breaks and emojis make bios readable at a glance
