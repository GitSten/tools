Hashtag Strategy for 2026

  • Mix tag sizes — combine mega tags (#fitness, 500M+), mid-tier (#homegym, 10M+) and niche tags (#garagegimsession, 50K) for best reach
  • TikTok: 3–5 tags — TikTok's algorithm is primarily content-driven. Over-tagging can hurt performance. Use 3–5 highly relevant tags
  • Instagram: 5–15 tags — Instagram indexes hashtags. Use a mix of sizes and always include at least 2 ultra-niche tags
  • Avoid banned tags — some tags are shadowbanned on Instagram. Check before use with a quick search in-app
