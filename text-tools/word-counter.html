Common Word Count Targets

Social Media

  • Tweet / X post: 280 characters maximum
  • Instagram caption: 2,200 characters (algorithm favours 125–150 chars for previews)
  • LinkedIn post: 3,000 characters; first 210 visible before "see more"
  • TikTok caption: 2,200 characters
  • Facebook post: 63,206 characters (no practical limit for most uses)

SEO Content

  • Meta description: 150–160 characters for optimal display in search results
  • Title tag: 50–60 characters
  • Blog post (informational): 1,500–2,500 words to compete for most keywords
  • Pillar content / long guide: 3,000–8,000+ words for competitive head terms

Academic Writing

  • Abstract: 150–300 words typically
  • Short essay: 500–1,000 words
  • Standard essay: 1,500–5,000 words
  • Dissertation: 10,000–100,000 words depending on level

Reading Time: How It's Calculated

Our reading time estimate uses an average adult reading speed of 210 words per minute — this is the median for silent reading of average-complexity prose. Technical content (code, academic writing, dense arguments) is typically read at 150–180 words/minute. Easy fiction reads at 250–300 words/minute. Our estimate is a useful benchmark, not a precise measurement.
