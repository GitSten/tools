Why we built this

Most online tools make you create an account before you can use them. Then they send marketing emails. Then they put the useful features behind a paywall. Then the QR codes you made stop working because you didn't upgrade.

We disagree with all of that. ToolsNowPro exists to prove that useful tools don't need to be complicated or extractive. The QR codes you generate here are static — they'll work for as long as the destination URL exists, with no subscription keeping them alive. The images you convert never leave your device. The passwords are generated locally and never transmitted anywhere.

We keep the site running through Google AdSense advertising. You see a few ads; we keep the tools free. That's the deal — nothing hidden.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What data we collect

ToolsNowPro collects minimal data. We do not require account registration and do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords through normal tool use.

When you use our tools, all processing happens in your browser. Text you enter into the case converter, images you load into the image converter, and QR code content are never transmitted to our servers.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand which pages are visited and how long visitors stay. This collects anonymised usage data including approximate location, browser type, and pages visited. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalised ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Cookies

We do not set cookies ourselves. Google Analytics and Google AdSense set their own cookies as described in Google's privacy policy. You can delete or block cookies through your browser settings.

Third parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties beyond Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which are described above.

Children

ToolsNowPro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
