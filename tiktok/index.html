TikTok Username Rules (2026)

📏 Max 24 Characters

Keep it shorter — 8–15 characters is ideal for memorability and display.

🔤 Letters, Numbers, _ and .

No spaces or special characters. Use underscores or dots to separate words.

🔄 Change Every 30 Days

TikTok lets you change your username once every 30 days. Choose wisely.

🔡 Case Insensitive

TikTok treats uppercase and lowercase the same. "LunarPetal" = "lunarpetal".
